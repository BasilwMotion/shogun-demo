What Shogun does on cement plants

Cement plants run hot, heavy and continuously — and their rotating equipment is exactly the territory where Shogun's specialist crews are at home. Our teams handle the precision work that keeps a kiln line turning: kiln alignment to bring the rotary kiln back onto its true axis, and complete kiln shell section replacements when heat and wear have taken their toll — including submerged arc welding for full-penetration shell joints.

Beyond the kiln itself, Shogun services heat exchangers, replaces separator rotors in the finish-grinding circuit, installs and repairs air ducting, and performs line boring on site so large components are machined in place rather than shipped away. We also work on converters & tipplers and provide experienced plant supervision for shutdowns, so every discipline on the job pulls in the same direction and the plant comes back online on schedule.

It's the same promise as everywhere else we work: do it the right way, the first time — with 24-hour availability when a kiln can't wait.

What Shogun does with concentrator plant equipment

Shogun's know-how extends well past single machines — through good relationships with customers and OEM suppliers, the company is known for the installation of entire concentrator plants. Although mills, crushers and winders are our core specialities, our teams carry a wide range of knowledge on the equipment that surrounds them: flotation cells, thickeners, pumps, compressors, rotary dryers, gearboxes and fans.

Day to day that means conveyor installation and maintenance, cyclone maintenance, girth gear run-out correction, lubrication units, stackers & reclaimers, heavy-lift hydraulic equipment, medium engineering fabrication and NDT inspections — the full circle of mechanical services a concentrator needs from commissioning through every shutdown after.

Our site teams — engineers, supervisors, riggers, fitters, boilermakers and alignment technicians — mobilise with the full Shogun fleet, so a project is executed safely, successfully and on time.
