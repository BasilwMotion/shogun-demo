Our Story

The History of Shogun

Three decades of steady growth — from a winder specialist to a full-circle mining construction partner trusted by the biggest names in the industry. (Click each milestone to expand)

1994

Shogun Construction is founded

The company is established in South Africa, initially specialising in winders — the critical hoisting machinery at the heart of every deep-level mine.

Late 1990s

Expansion into crushers & mills

Shogun soon broadens its activities to encompass all aspects of mining machinery, adding crushers and mills to its specialist portfolio.

2000s

A reputation is built

Shogun becomes well-known for its expertise, high-quality tooling, 24-hour availability and on-time service delivery — built on strong relationships with customers and suppliers.

2010s

Full-circle plant capability

Capability grows to cover kilns, cement plants and the installation of entire concentrator plants, backed by an extensive fleet of vehicles, machinery and specialist equipment.

Today

150 permanent specialists — and growing

Shogun employs a permanent staff complement of 150 well-experienced, highly skilled members, with the ability to enlarge its resources whenever a project timeline requires it.

Vision & Growth

The Next Big Step

Shogun is very driven to continue growing into a global presence along with our strategic partners. Here is how we plan to get there.

Growing into a global presence

Building on a proven track record across Southern Africa's gold, platinum and copper belts, Shogun's ambition is to take its specialist know-how to mining regions worldwide.

  • Expand from a Southern African footprint to international mining projects
  • Leverage 24-hour availability and rapid mobilisation as a competitive edge abroad
  • Scale the fleet — LDVs to low-bed trucks, cranes and manitous — to support remote sites
  • Enlarge resources on demand to comply with any project timeline, anywhere

Alongside our strategic partners

Shogun's growth is anchored in long-standing relationships with OEMs and mining houses.

  • Deepen OEM relationships with partners such as FLSmidth, Metso Outotec and ABB
  • Continue delivering for mining leaders — Anglo American, AngloGold Ashanti, Sibanye-Stillwater, Harmony Gold, First Quantum Minerals
  • Grow through good relationships with customers and suppliers — the way it's been done since 1994

Broadening full-circle capability

From single machines to entire plants — the goal is total project capability under one roof.

  • Full-circle solutions: installation, refurbishment, inspections and condition monitoring
  • Advanced techniques: vibration analysis, motion amplification, on-site machining, liner optimisation
  • Entire concentrator plant installations and complete cement plant services
  • Do it the right way, the first time — increasing equipment availability, decreasing maintenance cost

Investing in our people

What sets Shogun apart is a highly skilled, dedicated and talented team.

  • Maintain and grow the 150-strong permanent complement of specialists
  • Develop trades: riggers, fitters, boilermakers, alignment technicians, lance operators
  • Keep employee satisfaction a founding principle as the company scales
  • Focus on service delivery so customers experience optimal productivity
